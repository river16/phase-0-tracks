MACRHL is a fun recreational league for brand new to experienced players. You do not need to have had any hockey experience to play in this league.

  1. You do not need to have ever played hockey before
  2. You do not need to know all the rules
  3. You should have some experience in casual ice skating or roller blading
  4. You should generally be able to balance and glide well without falling

TEAMS ARE FORMED WITH PLAYERS IN THESE POSITIONS

Positions
Each Team
wing 4
center 2
defense 4
goalie 2

WANNA BE A GOALIE??

We have free learn to be a goalie clinics. Goalies play for free!! No experience necessary!!
